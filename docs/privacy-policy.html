Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

What we collect

  • Email address — when you create an account, used only for authentication
  • Location — used to find bathrooms near you, never stored on our servers
  • Reviews and bathroom submissions — content you voluntarily submit, stored in our database and visible to other users

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data
  • We don't share your data with third parties except as required to operate the app
  • We don't track you across other apps or websites

Third-party services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and database. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • OpenStreetMap — bathroom location data. No personal data is shared with OSM.

Your rights

You can delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.
